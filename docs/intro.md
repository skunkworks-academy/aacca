## Provisional placement

**Digital Transformation Practitioner — Business Operations and Process Optimisation**

- **Primary pathway:** integrated property operations, data-driven decision-making and low-code automation.
- **Secondary pathway:** digital product leadership and PropTech entrepreneurship.
- **Preferred delivery model:** improve and integrate existing tools first, then introduce custom capability only where justified.

## Purpose

This roadmap converts the available assessment and property-transformation discovery evidence into a sequenced plan for digital operations, cloud and data fundamentals, process automation, analytics, portfolio development, governance, security and measurable transformation outcomes.

## Development emphasis

The objective is not software engineering for its own sake. Alberto must develop the ability to diagnose operational friction, map processes, establish trustworthy data, configure practical automation, measure outcomes, communicate decisions and supervise implementation partners.

## One-year North Star

> By July 2027, Alberto should be able to lead a controlled property-operations transformation pilot, demonstrate an integrated data and workflow prototype, communicate measurable business value, and supervise implementation partners with credible cloud, automation, analytics and governance knowledge.

## Development principle

Every learning activity must connect to **one operational problem**, **one measurable outcome** and **one portfolio artefact**. Avoid accumulating disconnected course completions without applying them to the transformation pilot.

## Career direction

| Horizon | Outcome |
