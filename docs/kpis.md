# Goals, KPIs and Review Gates

## Development objectives

| Objective | Action | Measure | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Establish an integrated operating baseline | Map five priority processes and define authoritative sources. | Approved maps, system register, data dictionary and baseline KPIs. | 31 Aug 2026 |
| Build cloud and automation literacy | Complete AZ-900 and PL-900 learning and practical labs. | Practice scores, completion evidence and mentor validation. | 31 Oct 2026 |

## KPI definitions

### Process baseline completeness
Five high-value processes mapped; owners and service levels identified; controls and exception paths documented; baseline cycle time and quality recorded.

### Data governance readiness
Authoritative sources identified; critical entities and fields defined; ownership, sensitivity, retention and quality rules assigned; reconciliation procedure tested.

### Automation value
One low-risk workflow tested; approval, error, audit and rollback controls operate; user acceptance recorded; benefit measured against baseline.

### Reporting reliability
KPI definitions approved; refresh sequence documented; data-quality indicators visible; access aligns with role and least privilege.

### Portfolio evidence quality
Sanitised artefacts are reproducible; assumptions and limitations are explicit; version history and reviews are visible; decision pack is complete.

### Leadership and adoption
Decision rights and RACI approved; stakeholders receive updates; risks, decisions and benefit claims are documented; next-stage recommendation is clear.

## Review gates

| Gate | Decision questions |
|---|---|
| 30 days | Is the learning routine sustainable? Are the five processes and system inventory credible? |
| 60 days | Is the data baseline usable? Are app, flow and dashboard designs controlled and relevant? |
| 90 days | Does the first prototype solve a real problem safely? Is there evidence to approve a pilot? |
| 6 months | Has a workflow or dashboard created measurable value? Are architecture decisions improving? |
| 9 months | Can Alberto lead stakeholder decisions, risk management and operating-model communication? |
| 12 months | Is there enough evidence to scale, pursue advanced credentials or commercialise a service? |
