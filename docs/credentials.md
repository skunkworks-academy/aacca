## Formal exam rule

Schedule AZ-900 or PL-900 only when the official path is complete, a practice assessment indicates readiness, a relevant lab artefact exists, Raydo Matthee approves the attempt and the exam remains active.

## Evidence required for every credential

- official transcript, badge or profile link;
- completion date and provider;
- short learning reflection;
- one applied example;
- sanitised evidence link;
- mentor validation where required.
