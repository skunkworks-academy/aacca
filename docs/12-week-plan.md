# 12-Week IDR Execution Plan

<div className="idr-badges"><span className="idr-badge">6–8 hours per week</span><span className="idr-badge">25 credential targets</span><span className="idr-badge">Fortnightly mentor review</span><span className="idr-badge">Applied evidence required</span></div>

## Operating rules

- Business-critical operational work takes precedence.
- Do not run more than two active learning projects and one formal exam-preparation stream simultaneously.
- A badge is evidence of learning, not operational competence by itself.
- Every week must end with a sanitised artefact, decision or measurable improvement.
- Formal exam attempts remain subject to practice readiness and mentor approval.

## Weekly plan

| Week | Theme | Credential targets | Lab / tutorial | Assignment | Evidence gate |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | Validate and frame | Enterprise Design Thinking Practitioner; Data Literacy | Stakeholder mapping and IDR validation | Select five priority processes | Approved scope and study schedule |
| Week 2 | Plan and govern | Agile Explorer; Project Management Fundamentals; Getting Started with Data | SIPOC and current-state mapping | Build system inventory and project board | Process candidates and system register |
| Week 3 | Cloud foundations | Describe cloud concepts; Azure architecture and services | Azure sandbox and cloud decision brief | Build data-source inventory and ownership matrix | Cloud brief and source map |
| Week 4 | Cloud governance | Azure management and governance; AZ-900 readiness | Identity, cost, monitoring and governance | Create data dictionary and KPI ownership | Practice evidence and baseline controls |
| Week 5 | Power Platform foundation | Platform business value; Power Apps; Power Automate | Build a controlled approval flow | Map maintenance or rental reminder workflow | Approval path and audit plan |
| Week 6 | Apps and analytics | Power BI; business process mapping; Canvas Apps preparation | Canvas app and dashboard starter | Build intake form and dashboard wireframe | User stories, model and wireframe |
| Week 7 | Data quality | Data Fundamentals; Canvas Apps assessment | Power Query cleaning and reconciliation | Build integrated synthetic property dataset | Data-quality report and app evidence |
| Week 8 | Process mining | Introduction, use and manage process mining | Synthetic event log and mining canvas | Analyse bottlenecks and rework | Findings and improvement backlog |
| Week 9 | Operational reporting | Process Mining Power BI report; Cloud Computing Fundamentals | Dashboard v1 and refresh procedure | Write executive KPI narrative | Dashboard and reporting runbook |
| Week 10 | Security and privacy | Cybersecurity Fundamentals | POPIA classification and least privilege review | Create risk register and access model | Privacy and security control record |
| Week 11 | Governed AI and automation | Getting Started with AI; Power Automate Applied Skill | Approved-data automation or assistant proof of concept | Test human approval, failure and rollback | Test results and limitations |
| Week 12 | Assessment and decision | PL-900 readiness; evidence consolidation | Capstone demonstration and retrospective | Present pilot recommendation | Portfolio pack and next-quarter plan |

## Review cadence

- **End of Week 2:** scope, availability and baseline validation.
- **End of Week 4:** 30-day cloud, process and data baseline review.
- **End of Week 6:** first app, flow and dashboard design review.
- **End of Week 8:** 60-day process-mining and prototype review.
- **End of Week 10:** governance, privacy and security review.
- **End of Week 12:** 90-day capstone review and go/no-go decision.

## Completion thresholds

| Rating | Requirement |
|---|---|
| On track | 18 of 25 credential targets, 80% weekly evidence, no critical privacy breaches |
| Target achieved | 22 of 25 targets, 90% weekly evidence, one tested dashboard and one controlled workflow |
| Stretch achieved | 25 of 25 targets, AZ-900 and PL-900 passed, applied portfolio reviewed |
| Hold | Unauthorised data use, exposed personal data, unsupported benefit claims or ignored mentor controls |
