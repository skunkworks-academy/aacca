# Course Curriculum and Learning Modules

Each module combines theory, a guided tutorial, a practical lab, an assignment, evidence and mentor review.

| Module | Topic | Learning outcomes | Practical output |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | IDR validation and transformation framing | Confirm scope, role, constraints, stakeholders and benefits hypothesis. | Validation record and transformation charter. |
| 2 | Process discovery and service design | Use SIPOC, shadowing, current-state mapping, controls and exceptions. | Five process candidates and one approved map. |
| 3 | Data literacy and source governance | Define entities, sources, ownership, quality and privacy classification. | System register, source map and ownership matrix. |
| 4 | Cloud concepts and governance | Compare cloud models, Azure services, cost, identity, governance and monitoring. | Cloud decision brief and AZ-900 evidence. |
| 5 | Power Platform fundamentals | Understand Dataverse, connectors, Power Apps, Power Automate and Power BI. | Platform selection matrix. |
| 6 | Low-code app design | Translate user needs into stories, forms, screens, permissions and data operations. | Canvas app prototype and test script. |
| 7 | Data preparation and KPI design | Clean, reconcile and model property data; define operational KPIs. | Synthetic dataset, dictionary and quality report. |
| 8 | Process mining and improvement | Prepare event logs, identify rework and prioritise improvement candidates. | Mining report and improvement backlog. |
| 9 | Dashboard and executive reporting | Build interpretable metrics, refresh procedures and management narrative. | Executive dashboard v1 and reporting runbook. |
| 10 | Security, privacy and vendor governance | Apply POPIA, least privilege, consent, retention, audit and risk controls. | Privacy classification, access model and risk register. |
| 11 | Governed automation and AI | Implement approvals, error handling, human oversight, limitations and rollback. | Controlled automation or approved-data assistant proof of concept. |
| 12 | Pilot leadership and scale decision | Produce business case, RACI, roadmap, benefits baseline and adoption plan. | Capstone demonstration and 90-day recommendation. |

## Course completion requirements

A module is complete only when the learning content is completed, the lab is reproducible, the assignment meets acceptance criteria, evidence is stored safely, a reflection is recorded and the mentor reviews the defined gate.

## Recommended weekly cadence

| Activity | Weekly target | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| Structured learning | 2 hours | Credential-aligned cloud, data, automation and process modules. |
| Applied build work | 2–3 hours | Process mapping, data preparation, dashboard and workflow prototypes. |
| Documentation | 1 hour | Decisions, risks, README files, architecture notes and benefit evidence. |
| Stakeholder engagement | 45–60 minutes | Validate problems, service levels and usability. |
| Mentor review | 45 minutes fortnightly | Review quality, blockers, controls and next actions. |
