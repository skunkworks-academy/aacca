# Mentor and Facilitation Plan

## Assigned mentor and facilitator

**Raydo Matthee**

Raydo will act as Alberto’s digital-transformation mentor and facilitator. The role is to validate the development baseline, guide credential sequencing, review process/data/architecture/automation evidence, challenge unsupported benefit claims, enforce privacy boundaries, remove blockers and approve progression through review gates.

## Review cadence

- **Fortnightly mentor session:** 45 minutes.
- **30-day formal review:** baseline, learning routine and cloud/data evidence.
- **60-day formal review:** app, automation, dashboard and process-mining evidence.
- **90-day formal review:** pilot readiness and go/no-go recommendation.
- **Quarterly reviews thereafter:** objectives, credentials, projects and scale.

## First six mentor sessions

| Session | Focus |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Validate the IDR, role, weekly availability, current credentials and five highest-value processes. |
| 2 | Review system inventory, data sources, current-state maps and baseline KPIs. |
| 3 | Review cloud and Power Platform fundamentals; approve the first low-risk automation. |
| 4 | Review dashboard model, data-quality findings, privacy classification and access controls. |
| 5 | Review pilot charter, business case, risks, stakeholder adoption and implementation sequencing. |
| 6 | Assess 90-day evidence, select the next formal credential and approve the 6–12 month portfolio plan. |

## Standard session agenda

1. Progress against prior actions.
2. Evidence demonstration.
3. Blockers and risks.
4. Privacy and security check.
5. KPI and benefit review.
6. Decisions required.
7. Actions, owners and due dates.
8. Reflection and next learning target.

## Mentor approval record

Each formal gate should capture the date, evidence reviewed, score or decision, conditions, required corrections, next-stage approval and written acknowledgement.
